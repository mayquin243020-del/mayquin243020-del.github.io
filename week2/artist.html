Enhypen

Enhypen is a South Korean boy group formed by Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and Hybe Corporation through the survival show I-Land. The group has seven members: Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. They debuted on November 30, 2020, with their first extended play Border: Day One. The name “Enhypen” comes from the hyphen symbol (-), meaning connection, discovery, and growth. Just like a hyphen links words to create new meaning, Enhypen aims to connect, discover, and grow together as one team through music and performances.
