Enhypen

Enhypen is a South Korean boy band formed by Belift Lab . Formerly a joint venture betweent CJ ENM and Hybe Corporation , show I-land . The group consists of seven members : Heesung,Jay, Jake, Sunghoon ,Sunoo, Jungwon and Ni-ki. They debuted on November 30,2020 with extended play Border:Day One .
